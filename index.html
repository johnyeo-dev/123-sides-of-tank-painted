Application Question

Hassan had a rectangular block with a square base. He painted all faces and cut it into 1-cm cubes.

There were 68 cubes with exactly 2 faces painted. The height is 9 cm.

Find the volume of the block.

Guided Thinking

2 faces painted → edge cubes

Edges formula: 4(n−2) + 4(9−2)

Let base side = n

68 = 4(n−2) + 4(7)

68 = 4(n−2) + 28

40 = 4(n−2)

10 = n−2 → n = 12

Volume = 12 × 12 × 9 = 1296 cm³
